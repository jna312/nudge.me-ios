Support & User Guide

Welcome to nudge.me

nudge.me is a voice-powered reminder app. Create reminders by speaking naturally—no typing required.

Getting Started

  1. Open nudge.me
  2. Hold the microphone button
  3. Speak your reminder (e.g., "Call mom tomorrow at 3 PM")
  4. Release the button
  5. Your reminder is saved and you'll be notified at the scheduled time

Voice Commands

nudge.me understands natural language. Here are examples of what you can say:

Basic Reminders

  • "Call mom tomorrow at 3 PM"
  • "Meeting at 2 PM"
  • "Pick up groceries in 30 minutes"
  • "Dentist appointment next Monday at 9 AM"

Relative Times

  • "In 10 minutes"
  • "In 2 hours"
  • "Tonight at 8"
  • "Tomorrow morning"

Specific Dates

  • "January 20th at noon"
  • "Next Friday at 5 PM"
  • "This Saturday at 10 AM"

Early Warnings

  • "Meeting at 2 PM with a 15 minute warning"
  • "Doctor at 10 AM with an early alert"
  • "Call at 5 PM, warn me 30 minutes before"

Managing Reminders

Complete a Reminder

Tap the circle next to any reminder to mark it done.

Delete a Reminder

Swipe left on a reminder and tap Delete.

Snooze a Reminder

Swipe right on a reminder to snooze it for 10 minutes.

Undo

After creating a reminder, tap "Undo" within 5 seconds to remove it.

Typing Reminders

Prefer to type? Tap "Type instead" below the microphone button to enter reminders using the keyboard.

Siri Integration

Create reminders without opening the app:

  1. Say "Hey Siri, nudge me"
  2. Siri will ask what you want to be reminded about
  3. Speak your reminder
  4. Siri will ask when to remind you
  5. Speak the time
  6. Your reminder is saved in nudge.me

Calendar Sync

Sync your reminders to Apple Calendar:

  1. Open nudge.me
  2. Tap the gear icon (Settings)
  3. Enable "Sync with Calendar"
  4. Grant calendar access when prompted

Your reminders will appear in a calendar called "nudge.me Reminders" in Apple Calendar. Reminders sync automatically when you add, edit, delete, or complete them.

Home Screen Widget

Add a widget to see your upcoming reminders at a glance:

  1. Long-press on your Home Screen
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top corner
  3. Search for "nudge.me"
  4. Choose a widget size
  5. Tap "Add Widget"

Settings

Access Settings by tapping the gear icon in the app.

Daily Closeout

Set a time to review uncompleted reminders at the end of each day.

Calendar Integration

Enable or disable syncing reminders to Apple Calendar.

Default Early Warning

Set a default heads-up notification time for all reminders (5, 15, 30 minutes, or 1 hour before).

Writing Style

Choose how reminder text appears: Sentence case, Title Case, or ALL CAPS.

Notifications

nudge.me sends notifications to remind you of scheduled items.

If you're not receiving notifications:
  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap Notifications
  3. Find nudge.me
  4. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  5. Check that Alerts, Sounds, and Badges are turned on

iCloud Sync

Your reminders automatically sync across all your Apple devices using iCloud.

Requirements

  • iCloud must be enabled on your device
  • You must be signed into the same Apple ID on all devices
If reminders aren't syncing:
  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap iCloud
  4. Ensure nudge.me is enabled for iCloud

Troubleshooting

Microphone Not Working

  1. Open iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone
  2. Ensure nudge.me is enabled
  3. Restart the app

Speech Not Recognized

  1. Open iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition
  2. Ensure nudge.me is enabled
  3. Speak clearly and avoid background noise
  4. Make sure you include AM or PM when saying times

Reminders Not Saving

  • Make sure you speak a time or date (e.g., "at 3 PM" or "tomorrow")
  • nudge.me will ask for clarification if it doesn't understand

App Crashing

  1. Force quit the app (swipe up from app switcher)
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Reinstall the app if the issue persists

Privacy

nudge.me respects your privacy:

  • Your reminders are stored on your device and your personal iCloud account
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to external servers
  • Voice input is processed using Apple's Speech Recognition and is not recorded
  • We do not track your usage or show ads
